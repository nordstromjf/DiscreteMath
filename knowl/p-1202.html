To disprove a property, find a specific counterexample:

  • Reflexive.
    Find \((x, x)\notin R\) for some \(x\in A\text{.}\)
  • Symmetric.
    Find \((x, y)\in R\) with \((y, x)\notin R\text{.}\)
  • Transitive.
    Find \((x, y), (y, z)\in R\) with \((x, z)\notin R\text{.}\)
