Hint: Our statement has the form \(\forall \epsilon, \exists \delta, P(\delta)\rightarrow Q(\epsilon).\) So the negation has the form \(\exists \epsilon, \forall \delta, P(\delta)\wedge \sim Q(\epsilon).\)
