Consider the statement “For all llamas, \(L\text{,}\) in Discrete Math, \(L\) is getting an A.” The negation is “There exists a llama, \(L\text{,}\) in Discrete Math, such that \(L\) is not getting an A.” Since no such llama exists, the negation is false. Making the original true. So every llama in Discrete is getting a A.
