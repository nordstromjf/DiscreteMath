Conversely, if we have a partition \(\{A_1, A_2, \ldots, A_n, \ldots\}\) of \(A\) and define the relation \((a, b)\in R \Leftrightarrow a\) and \(b\) are in the same subset \(A_i\text{,}\) then \(R\) is an equivalence relation. We call this the equivalence relation induced by the partition.
